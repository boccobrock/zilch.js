Rules:
Players roll the dice, scoring them as follows:
1 = 100
5 = 50
Three 1's = 1000
Three 2's = 200
Three 3's = 300
Three 4's = 400
Three 5's = 500
Three 6's = 600
3 Pairs = 1000
Straight = 1500
Each roll must have some value, otherwise it is a zilch and the player loses all their points.
Setting aside the dice that are being score-labeld, the player may continue to roll the other dice to earn more points.
If a player rolls the dice and all the dice have value, the player may keep their points and roll all 6 dice again.
Rolling a zilch at any point causes the player to lose all points earned that turn.
If a player is not yet on the score-labelboard, they must earn at least 500 points to save their score-label.
After a player goes above 10000 points, the other players have 1 turn to earn more points, whoever has the most points then wins.
